About us

Josh and Jerry's Ice Cream is the world's sweetest chain of ice cream specialty shops, providing guests with a wide array of ice cream flavors and delicious treats at more than 300 retail shops in more than 50 countries around the world. The brand was founded by Josh, an ice cream enthusiasts whose passion for ice cream led him to the creation of many iconic ice cream flavors including PB&J, Apple Pie ala mode and Banana French Toast. Today, Josh's Ice Cream Shop has 36 ice creams in its flavor library, and also offers custom ice creams and frozen beverages. His shops serve as places where people can connect and create special memories while they explore a wide array of flavors, including a new Flavor of the Month every month. He is headquartered in California.
